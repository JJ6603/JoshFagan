My Works

FuturesX

Website and Application Design

FututresX is an Education Startup company that aims to redefine how education is delivered to students in years 7-10 across Australia.
FuturesX is part of a new wave of LMS based education, focusing on linking skills students learn in school with real life applications and industries.

As part of my work with FuturesX I conducted research into education and classroom engagement, and brainstormed how this could be incorperated into the Website.
I also created several design sketeches for potential web page layouts, which were then used to create low, medium and high fidelity prototypes
using pen and paper, Figma, and Marvel App.

QUT Wellbeing App

App Re-Design

The QUT Wellbeing app was developed as a way for students to better manage their studies and also look after their mental health.
The app is free for all QUT students and consists of a daily planner, meditaion videos, and other content related to mental health
as well as a boking feature for the QUT councelling service

My work with the QUT Wellbeing App included interviewing 3 students and asking them to keep a diary as they used the app in their day to day lives.
this helped me to identify several issues with the usability of the app and the overall user experience.
As a result of this I created several low fidelity prototype sketches of potential improvements for the app.

Study Eyes

HCI Research Project

As part of my final year of university, I was given a research project in the area of Students Experience with procrastination.
This research involved getting hands on experience with emerging technologies such as AI, Humanoid Robots, Augmented Reality and IoT.
It also involved interviewing and setting up observation sessions with 5 research participants in order to better understand study and procrastination habits.
Using this data I formed a Field Theory around procrastination, and then designed a fictional product aimed at highlighting the issues surrounding procrastination.

That solution was Study Eyes, a pair of augmented reality glasses that would highlight potential distractions in the User's surroundings,
and can create a persons ideal study environment (library, classroom, or home study) around them in order to minimise procrastination.
I then had to evaluate this product with another 3 research participants which included using several interview techniques and another 5-day observation diary.
